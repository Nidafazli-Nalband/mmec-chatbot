Welcome to MMEC

Maratha Mandal Engineering College (MMEC), Belagavi, was established in 1997. It is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU). The college is committed to providing quality education in engineering and technology.

The campus spans over acres with modern infrastructure, fostering a vibrant academic environment. MMEC emphasizes holistic development, combining academic excellence with extracurricular activities, research, and industry collaborations.

Our mission is to produce skilled engineers who contribute to society and the nation. With a dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities, MMEC prepares students for successful careers in engineering.
