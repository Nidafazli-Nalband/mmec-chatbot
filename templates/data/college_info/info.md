# Maratha Mandal Engineering College (MMEC)

Welcome to Maratha Mandal Engineering College (MMEC), Belagavi, established in 1997. MMEC is approved by AICTE and affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU). The campus offers a vibrant academic environment with modern facilities.

## Admissions
Admissions at MMEC are based on KCET, COMEDK, and JEE Main scores. Eligibility requires passing 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics with a minimum aggregate. Applications open annually during the counseling period. Visit the official admissions page or contact the admissions office for the latest forms and dates.

## Courses
MMEC offers undergraduate programs in:
- Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)
- Robotics & Artificial Intelligence (R&AI)
- Mechanical Engineering
- Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

Postgraduate and doctoral programs are also available in engineering disciplines.

## Facilities
The college provides extensive facilities including:
- Well-equipped laboratories and workshops
- Spacious classrooms and seminar halls
- Large library
- Hostels for UG and PG students
- Sports grounds for various games
- Medical facilities and campus Wi-Fi

## Placements
MMEC’s placement cell supports students with career counseling, training, internships, and campus recruitment. Top recruiters include Infosys, TCS, Accenture, Bosch, among others, with placement rates approximately 80-90%.

## Events
MMEC hosts academic seminars, cultural festivals, sports meets, workshops, and guest lectures throughout the year. Updates on upcoming events are available on the college website.

## About
Managed by Maratha Mandal, Belagavi (established 1931), MMEC focuses on academic excellence, discipline, and ethical values. The college aims to develop skilled professionals with a strong societal commitment.
