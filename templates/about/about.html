About MMEC

Managed by Maratha Mandal, Belagavi (established in 1931), MMEC focuses on academic excellence, discipline, and ethical values. The college aims to develop skilled professionals with a strong societal commitment.

Our Vision

To be a premier engineering institution that nurtures innovation, excellence, and social responsibility.

Our Mission

To provide quality education, foster research and development, and contribute to the socio-economic development of the region.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, and Social Commitment.

College History

Established in 1997, MMEC has grown from a small institution to a prominent engineering college in Karnataka. Affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines.

Management

The college is managed by Maratha Mandal, Belagavi, a charitable trust with a legacy of over 90 years in education and social service.
