Admissions

Admissions at MMEC are based on entrance exam scores from KCET, COMEDK, and JEE Main. We offer undergraduate programs in various engineering disciplines.

Eligibility Criteria

Passed 10+2 examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects
Minimum aggregate marks as per university guidelines
Valid score in KCET/COMEDK/JEE Main

Admission Process

Appear for entrance examination (KCET/COMEDK/JEE Main)
Participate in counseling process
Submit required documents and fees
Complete admission formalities

Important Dates

Admissions open annually during the counseling period. Please check the official websites of KCET, COMEDK, and JEE Main for the latest schedules.
